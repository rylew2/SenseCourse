A simple tool to help you choose an OMSCS specialization as well as the courses for you to be successful in.

  • Step 1. Enter a new username and password here to register.
  • Step 2. Answer a question and let our AI suggest a specialization. You will get to pick the specialization you want.
  • Step 3. Once specialization is chosen, SenseCourse will generate 10 OMSCS courses for you to take based on OMSCentral course rating, your "Personality Insights" generated, and your preferred time commitment per week .
