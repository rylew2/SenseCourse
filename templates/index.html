#SenseCourse #Specialiazations #FindClasses #OMSCS #AI

A simple tool to help you choose an OMSCS specialization as well as the courses for you to be successful in.

  • Step 1. Perform a 5 second registration.
  • Step 2. Answer a simple question on "Why you picked Computer Science as your Major" in a few sentences.
  • Step 3. "Personality Insights" about you will be generated based on how you responded to the question.
  • Step 4. Our AI will propose specializations in order of best match based on your "Personality Insights". Choose whichever on you prefer and click submit.
  • Step 5. Write how many hours you want to commit to OMSCS each week and click submit.
  • Step 6. Based on rating, your "Personality Insights", and your time commitment, the courses you need to take will be generated.
